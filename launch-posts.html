Title
I'm an IT guy who built a brain-sharpening app in my spare time. It's not what I imagined yet, but it's real.
Body (copy everything below)
Quick background — I work in IT, mostly production support and builds monitoring. The kind of job where things break at 2am and you have to think fast. In my time off I play games that make me think, watch F1 and recently got into making coffee. I'm not a startup person, I just had an idea that wouldn't leave me alone. The idea was simple: what if there was an app that actually challenged you to think — not just tested if you remembered something, but pushed you to reason through it? And what if the AI didn't just give you the answer but asked you questions until you got there yourself? That's the Socratic method and it genuinely works, I just wanted to see if I could build it. So I did. It's called CogniChain. It's got daily problems across Math, Science, Logic, Puzzles and Aptitude. Each one has an AI guide (built on Claude) that refuses to give you the answer — it just keeps asking you questions until you find it. You earn points, build streaks, there's a leaderboard. Honest disclaimer: the UI needs work and this isn't the full version I have in my head. The complete idea is bigger. But I showed a rough version to some friends and they loved it, so I figured I'd just ship what's working and build from here. You can try 5 problems without signing up: cognichain-h51n.vercel.app Tell me what's broken, what's confusing, what you'd actually use. I can take it.

Title (exact format matters)
Show HN: CogniChain – a problem-solving app where the AI asks you questions instead of giving answers
Body
I'm an IT professional. In between production incidents I built this. CogniChain is a daily problem-solving platform — Math, Science, Logic, Puzzles, Aptitude. The AI guide on each problem is built on Claude with one hard rule in the system prompt: never give the answer. Ask questions until the user finds it themselves. 48 problems live, daily challenges rotate automatically. Points, streaks, leaderboard, social feed. Guest mode works without signup — 5 problems, 3 guide messages per problem. Stack: FastAPI + Supabase + React + Claude API on Railway and Vercel. I'll be honest — the UI needs work and this isn't the final version I have in mind. Shipping it anyway because the core mechanic works and I want real feedback. cognichain-h51n.vercel.app
